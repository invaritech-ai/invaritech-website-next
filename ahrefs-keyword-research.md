## Strategic read

The strongest Finance Ops opportunity is not the phrase `Finance Ops` itself. It is the AP/invoice workflow vocabulary that searchers already use:

- Broad market terms: `automated invoice processing`, `invoice automation`, `invoice processing automation`, `accounts payable automation`.
- Easier control/workflow terms: `three way match`, `invoice approval workflow`, `invoice exception management`, `accounts payable controls`, `duplicate payment prevention`, `supplier statement reconciliation`.
- Xero-specific terms exist, but should be used as modifiers around a real Xero workflow page or tool, not as the whole SEO foundation.
- RegOps should remain visible as a second pillar, but current publishing priority should be Finance Ops because the keyword universe is larger and closer to the Xero/AP product direction.

## Publishing priority

1. Strengthen and interlink the existing invoice-processing and invoice-extractor assets.
2. Make the three-way matcher visible as a proper tool and support it with a glossary-style guide.
3. Create or strengthen an `invoice approval workflow` page with flowchart/template sections.
4. Use the supplier payment control rule table as the AP-controls anchor page.
5. Add Xero AP automation content only where the product claim is true and demonstrable.
6. Add duplicate-payment and supplier-statement tools later as free-traffic assets.
7. Keep RegOps alive through EUDR and regulatory reporting content, but do not let it dilute Finance Ops publishing.

## What not to lead with

- `finance operations automation` as the primary SEO keyword. It fits the brand, but Ahrefs shows low exact demand.
- `regulatory operations automation` as the primary RegOps keyword. It has very low exact demand.
- Broad BOFU software-comparison terms before there is enough proof, product clarity, and recurring free usage.
- `payment diversion fraud` as a first cluster. It is relevant, but the SERP intent is broader than supplier-payment controls.
- Generic AI automation language. The stronger keyword universe is AP workflow, invoice processing, controls, matching, approval, exception handling, and reconciliation.
