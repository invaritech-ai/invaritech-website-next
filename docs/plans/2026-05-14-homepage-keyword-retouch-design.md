# Homepage Keyword Retouch Design

Date: 2026-05-14

## Goal

Retouch homepage copy to align with validated keyword demand while preserving the current design language, conversion flow, and founder-led positioning.

## Constraints

- Do not degrade the existing visual design or section structure.
- Keep the H1 semantic text clean and crawler-safe.
- Avoid keyword stuffing; use natural language and buyer intent terms.
- Keep the homepage differentiated from generic AP software pages.

## SEO Positioning Decision

Primary homepage keyword target:

- `accounts payable controls`

Secondary homepage support terms:

- `invoice approval workflow`
- `invoice exception management`
- `duplicate payments`
- `supplier payment controls`
- `supplier statement reconciliation`
- `freight invoice audit`
- `payment approval checks`

Positioning language remains:

- Founder-led payment control design
- Without changing systems

## Copy Direction

### Hero

- Keep semantic H1 as:
  - `Clean up payment controls without changing systems.`
- Retouch lead paragraph to explicitly include AP + workflow control terminology.
- Keep support line and CTA pair intact.

### Problem Section

- Emphasize common operational breakpoints:
  - invoice approval workflow gaps
  - supplier statement reconciliation issues
  - freight invoice audit misses
  - duplicate vendor payments
- Preserve current “daily symptoms” narrative.

### Question Block

- Keep it direct and operational:
  - mention supplier payment controls
  - mention invoice exceptions and auditability

### How It Works

- Keep four-step structure.
- Explicitly mention:
  - invoice exception management
  - payment approval checks
  - evidence capture and audit trails

### Final CTA

- Keep current asset-first CTA strategy.
- Expand supporting sentence to include:
  - supplier payment controls
  - invoice exception routing
  - payment approval checks

## Additional Related Fixes in Same Pass

- Align OG and Twitter descriptions to the stronger homepage meta wording.
- Remove Careers from footer navigation (page can remain noindex).
- Reframe `/work/` metadata away from generic “AI Automation Case Studies” toward payment-control proof language.

## Out of Scope

- Full redesign of `/work/` page layout.
- New pages for keyword clusters.
- Broader blog/resource production sprint.

## Success Criteria

- Homepage copy naturally includes target terms without awkward phrasing.
- H1 semantic output is clean text only.
- Metadata descriptions are consistent across standard, OG, and Twitter.
- Footer no longer links `/careers/`.
- `/work/` no longer contradicts homepage positioning in metadata.
