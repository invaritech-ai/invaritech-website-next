# EUDR / RegOps Research Notes

## Primary angle

Use EUDR as the Europe-specific case study, but frame the article around working in a regulatory environment:

- regulated work is a system, not a filing task
- manual handling breaks under volume, audit pressure, and exception handling
- RegOps is the operating model that makes compliance repeatable

This is not an Australia-first search target. Treat EUDR as a European authority topic and keep the broader cluster anchored on compliance automation and regulatory operations.
