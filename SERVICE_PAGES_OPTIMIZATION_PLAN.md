# Service Pages Optimization Plan
*Based on mentor feedback and keyword analysis*

## Executive Summary
Your service pages are 85% there according to mentor feedback. This plan addresses the 15% gap by:
1. **Clarifying differentiation** between overlapping pages (AI Automation, Workflow Automation, AI Integration)
2. **Sharpening search intent alignment** so each page owns one primary keyword cluster
3. **Adding commercial anchors** for enterprise buyers (investment range, timelines, "best for" guidance)
4. **Optimizing internal linking** to create a semantic cluster that guides prospects through your maturity ladder

## 1. Current State Analysis

### Strengths (Per Mentor Assessment)
- **Strategic structure is smart**: AI PoC → Workflow Automation → AI Integration → Generative AI Dev → AI Automation → AI Chatbot
- **Matches enterprise maturity ladder** and budget expansion ladder
- **Finance-first framing** is strong
- **Generative AI page** is excellent from a positioning standpoint
- **PoC page** is very strong BOFU (bottom-of-funnel)
- **Chatbot page** works well as a low-ticket funnel

### Critical Issues to Address
1. **Overlap Problem**: 3 pages partially overlap in keyword intent:
   - AI Automation Services
   - Workflow Automation Consulting  
   - AI Integration Services

2. **SEO Blur**: To Google, these can appear similar. Need differentiation by **search intent**, not philosophy.

3. **Missing Commercial Anchors**: Enterprise buyers look for "best for teams with X", investment range, typical timeline, what happens after.

### Current Overlap Areas:

## 4. Differentiation Strategy (Per Mentor Feedback)

### AI Automation Services (`ai-automation-consulting/`)
**Primary Intent**: "replace manual workflows with AI"
**Target Keywords**:
- `ai automation services`
- `business ai automation` 
- `enterprise ai automation`
- `ai process automation`
**Outcome Language**: throughput, headcount leverage, SLA improvement
**What It Is NOT**: NOT integration plumbing

### Workflow Automation Consulting (`ai-workflow-automation-services/`)
**Primary Intent**: "process optimization + automation strategy"
**Target Keywords**:
- `workflow automation consulting`
- `process automation consulting`
- `business process automation consulting`
**Outcome Language**: Less AI hype. More operational efficiency.
**What It Is NOT**: NOT GenAI backend engineering

### AI Integration Services (`ai-integration-services/`)
**Primary Intent**: "connect AI to ERP/CRM safely"
**Target Keywords**:
- `ai integration services`
- `ai system integration`
- `generative ai integration`
- `ai tool integration`
**Outcome Language**: Sovereignty, VPC, auditability, vendor control
**What It Is NOT**: NOT workflow optimization

## 5. Copy Improvement Plan (Page-by-Page)

### Page 1: AI Automation Services (`ai-automation-consulting/`)
**Current Issue**: Too consulting-focused, not enough execution clarity
**Improvements**:
1. **Headline**: Shift from "AI Automation Consulting" to "AI Automation Services" (match search intent)
2. **Subheadline**: Emphasize "replace manual workflows with AI" outcome
3. **Add Commercial Anchors**:
   - "Best for teams with: [specific scenarios]"
   - "Typical investment: £Xk–£Yk for [specific outcome]"
   - "Timeline: Z weeks to measurable ROI"
4. **Clarify Scope**: Explicitly state "We don't do integration plumbing or workflow strategy"
5. **CTA Alignment**: Focus on "Get Execution Plan" vs "Book Consulting Call"

### Page 2: Workflow Automation Consulting (`ai-workflow-automation-services/`)
**Current Issue**: Too much AI focus, not enough pure process optimization
**Improvements**:
1. **Headline**: Emphasize "Process Optimization" alongside "Automation"
2. **Subheadline**: "Remove handoffs, approvals, and reconciliation drag"
3. **De-emphasize AI**: Lead with deterministic automation, mention AI as enhancement
4. **Add Commercial Anchors**:
   - "Best for COOs needing to scale throughput without linear headcount"
   - "Typical ROI: X% reduction in cycle time within Y weeks"
5. **Differentiate**: Clear statement: "This is NOT GenAI backend engineering"

### Page 3: AI Integration Services (`ai-integration-services/`)
**Current Issue**: Good, but needs stronger sovereignty messaging
**Improvements**:
1. **Headline**: Strengthen "Sovereign Data" positioning
2. **Subheadline**: "Connect AI to your ERP/CRM without surrendering control"
3. **Emphasize Governance**: Lead with VPC, on-prem, audit logs
4. **Add Commercial Anchors**:
   - "Best for regulated industries or data-sensitive enterprises"
   - "Deployment options: VPC, on-prem, or managed service"
5. **Differentiate**: Clear statement: "This is NOT workflow optimization"

### Page 4: Generative AI Development (`generative-ai-backend-development/`)
**Current Status**: Excellent positioning (per mentor)
**Minor Improvements**:
1. **Headline**: Consider "Generative AI Backend Engineering Services"
2. **Add Commercial Anchors**:
   - "Best for teams scaling from prototype to production"
   - "Typical engagement: £Xk+ for hardened microservices"
3. **Strengthen Technical Credibility**: Add more architecture diagrams/technical details

### Page 5: AI Chatbot Development (`enterprise-ai-chatbot-deployment/`)
**Current Status**: Good as low-ticket funnel
**Improvements**:
1. **Headline**: Consider "AI Chatbot Development Services" (match keyword)
2. **Emphasize "Fast Win"**: Lead with time-to-value (days/weeks, not months)
3. **Add Commercial Anchors**:
   - "Best for immediate customer support or internal knowledge lookup"
   - "Starting at £Xk for governed deployment"
4. **Multilingual Focus**: Highlight Cantonese, Mandarin, Bahasa as differentiators

### Page 6: AI PoC Development (`ai-automation-sprint/`)
**Current Status**: Very strong BOFU
**Minor Improvements**:
1. **Headline**: "AI Proof of Concept Development Services" (exact match keyword)
2. **Add Commercial Anchors**:
   - "Fixed price: £8k–£12k for 30-day validated wedge"
   - "Best for: [specific roles/scenarios]"
3. **Strengthen Risk Reversal**: Emphasize "go/no-go after diagnostic"
